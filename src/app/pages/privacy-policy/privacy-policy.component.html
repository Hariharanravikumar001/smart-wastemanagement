Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of personal information we may collect include:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and profile details.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our platform, including pickup history and environmental impact scores.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect from all our services to provide, maintain, protect, and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect WasteZero and our users. For example, we use your address to schedule and route our volunteers efficiently for waste pickups.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations, and individuals outside of WasteZero unless one of the following circumstances applies:

  • With your consent: We will share personal information with outside organizations when we have your consent.
  • For external processing: We provide personal information to our trusted businesses or persons to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy.
  • For legal reasons: We will share personal information if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation, or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.

4. Security

We work hard to protect WasteZero and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold. We use encryption to keep your data private while in transit and review our information collection, storage, and processing practices.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your data. You can exercise these rights by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@wastezero.com.
