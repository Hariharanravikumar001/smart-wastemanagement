About WasteZero

We are on a mission to transform waste management through technology, community engagement, and intelligent scheduling.

WasteZero connects citizens who want to dispose of waste responsibly with volunteers and organizations dedicated to processing it. Our platform ensures that recyclable, organic, and even hazardous waste finds its way to the right facilities, minimizing environmental impact.
